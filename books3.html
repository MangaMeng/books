My School 🏫

School ek jagah nahi, ek duniya thi — hamari apni duniya!

Har din ek nayi kahani hoti thi. Kabhi hasi, kabhi punishment, kabhi crush ki ek jhalak.

Main, Rahul, aur Ramesh teen dost — inseparable!

Assembly ke waqt hamesha line ke last mein khade hote, jahan teacher ki nazar kam padti thi.

Maths ka period toh aafat tha. Sir ka naam tha — "Tiwari Sir" — unka chalk ka missile kabhi bhi udh jaata.

Ek baar Rahul ne Tiwari Sir ki kursi hata di thi... jaise hi wo baithe — seedha floor! 😂

Agle din poore class ko punishment mila — “Do ghante tak hands-up.”

Lunch break? Ajeeb combination — Aalu chips + Amul Kool + chutney! Maza hi kuch aur tha.

School ke canteen ka samosa — duniya ka best tha... ya shayad bhukh zyada thi.

Crush ki baat? Haan, 8B ki Riya… sirf ek baar usne “pen” maanga tha — main toh hawa mein ud gaya tha!

Annual day function — jab main joker bana tha... aur sabko hasa diya tha.

Ek din PT sir ne bola: “Tumhare andar sportsmanship hai.”

Wahin se confidence build hua… aur school race jeeta tha main!

Farewell ka din aaya... sab aankhon mein aansu lekar mil rahe the.

Ramesh ne kaha: “School toh khatam ho gaya... dosti nahi.”

Teacher ne bola: “Yeh school tumhara base hai, yahan se upar jaana hai.”

Us din realise hua — school ek building nahi, ek feeling hai.

Har ek bench, har ek corner, har ek line — yaadgar hai.

Aaj bhi WhatsApp pe group active hai — “SCHOOL LEGENDS 2015” 😄

Life aage badh gayi hai, par dil school mein hi kahi hai.

Jab bhi school ke saamne se guzarta hoon, ek muskaan zarur aati hai.
