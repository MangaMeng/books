I Know You

Ek baar ka zikr hai, Mumbai ki crowded life mein do log mile — Amir aur Meera.

College ki library mein pehli nazar padhi thi ek dusre par.

Amir ek silent, thoughtful ladka tha — Meera ek chirpy aur creative ladki.

Unke beech zyada baatein nahi hoti thi, magar aankhon mein ek alag sa connection tha.

Ek din Meera ne chhoti si smile di, Amir ne palak jhapka kar jawab diya.

Dheere dheere, dosti shuru hui — uss dosti mein khamoshi zyada thi, shor kam.

Station par milte, ek hi coffee shop mein baithte, lekin kabhi ek dusre se zyada nahi poocha.

Phir ek din Meera ne kaha, “Tum ajeeb ho… par ache ajeeb.”

Amir bas muskura diya — uske liye woh kaafi tha.

Dono ke beech kuch na kuch tha — shabdon se pare, par dil ke kareeb.

College khatam hua, aur ek project ke liye Amir ko Delhi jaana pada.

Meera ne bus itna kaha, “Jab wapas aao, coffee pe chalte hain.”

Amir gaya… aur fir ek accident hua.

Hospital mein aankhein khuli, sab yaad tha — bas Meera ka chehra nahi.

Doctors ne bataya, kuch memory erase ho gayi hai.

Amir normal ho gaya, par uske dil mein ek khali jagah thi.

Usse lagta tha kuch important bhool gaya hai — par kya?

Meera ne try kiya contact karna, par Amir kisi Anjali naam ki ladki ke saath reh raha tha.

Usne socha, “Shayad uski zindagi mein jagah nahi rahi.”

Waqt beeta… kuch saal guzr gaye… sheher badal gaye… log badal gaye.

Ek art gallery mein ek painting lagi thi — naam tha "Unfinished Memory".

Amir wahan gaya tha sirf timepass ke liye — par us painting ko dekhte hi kuch to toota.

Us painting mein ek ladki thi, jo uske dil ko chhoo gayi.

Usne poocha, “Ye kisne banayi?” — jawab aaya, “Meera.”

Naam suna… kuch to jag gaya andar… ek choti si awaaz: “Tum use jaante ho.”

Dusri taraf Meera bhi wahan aayi thi, apni painting ki response dekhne.

Ek corner mein khadi thi, jab uski nazar Amir par padhi.

Woh ruk gayi… saans le li… samne gaya… aur aankhon mein aankhon dali.

Amir: “Shayad mai tumhe janta hoon...”

Meera: “Shayad mai tumhe janti hoon...”

Bas wahi pal tha… sab kuch keh diya… bina kehkar.

Na naam poocha, na purani baatein — sirf ek tasalli thi, ke dil ab shant tha.

Shayad yaad nahi tha, par connection ab bhi tha.

Kuch rishte yaadon se nahi, rooh se jude hote hain.

Wahi rishte… jo waqt, duri, aur yaadein bhi mita nahi sakti.

Woh dono alag ho gaye us gallery se — par dono ke chehre par ek hi muskan thi.

Ek muskan jo kehti thi… "I Know You."
